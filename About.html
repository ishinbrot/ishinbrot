Drexel Experience

I was a Peer Mentor/Student Ambassador for the College of Computing Informatics from Fall 2012 until I graduated in June 2016. I was also a Teaching Assistant for the Department of Computer Science from September 2012 until June 2016. In May 2016 I recieved the outstanding undergraduate Teaching Assistant Award.

I was a Teaching Assistant for CS 161, Introduction to Computing, CS 164, Introduction to Computer Science, CS 171 Programming I, CS 172 Programming II, CS 175 Programming I & II, and CS 275, Web and Mobile Application Development. As a teaching assistant I assisted students learn about the different aspects of computer science and programming. The classes that I helped with covered the following languages: C++, JAVA Android, Javascript, and HTML.

As a Peer Mentor I worked with the College of Computing Informatics to help prospective student get accustomed to college life at Drexel, as well as assisted in open houses and visit days.
